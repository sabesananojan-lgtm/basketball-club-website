About Our Club

Jaffna Club is a basketball club for players who love creative moves, strong teamwork, and bold improvement.

Our Mission

To help players build confidence, sharpen skills, and enjoy every moment on the court.

Our Values

Respect, dedication, teamwork, and fun shape how we train and play together.

Our Style
